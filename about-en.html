About ICX Technology

Our Company

ICX is a well-established, highly professional organization in 2019. The development of RF Communication and Embedded Sensor Networks, with a primary focus on Remote Sensing Technologies. Our strength lies in a foundation of highly dedicated designers, unwavering commitment to excellence, on-schedule delivery, and continuous performance improvement.

The core mission of ICX is to pioneer the development of advanced Remote & Automated Embedded Sensor Network solutions—both wired and wireless. Our technology integrates fundamental remote sensing applications with comprehensive data management and high-throughput information processing, powered by our robust backend Server Cluster infrastructure. This integration defines our core technical competencies and system architecture.

Driven by the goal of becoming a leader in Remote Sensor Network design and development, ICX has cultivated an impressive track record. We collaborate effectively with diverse local and international partners to deliver tailored Sensing Network solutions. Our portfolio encompasses Enterprise-grade, Industrial-grade, and High-reliability-grade applications, demonstrating our versatility and technical expertise.

Our Values

Innovate: At the core of our vision is relentless innovation. We push the boundaries of semiconductor design and manufacturing, creating next-generation processors, sensors, and SoCs that redefine performance, efficiency, and intelligence.

Connect: We engineer silicon that connects worlds. Our solutions seamlessly link devices, data, and people across IoT ecosystems, 5G networks, autonomous systems, and high-performance computing.

Xpertise: With deep domain knowledge in analog, digital, RF, and advanced packaging, our team masters the full stack of chip development to ensure quality, reliability, and scalability.
